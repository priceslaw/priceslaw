Plotting Your Course In Real Estate Law

 
 
 

For over a quarter century, the Price name has represented quality legal service in Santa Clara County.

PRICE LAW FIRM was built on this foundation and continues to provide innovative and intelligent solutions to our clients' challenging legal and business problems in a cost-effective manner.

PRICE LAW FIRM delivers creative and pragmatic strategies to overcome obstacles that arise in commercial and residential real estate. Our practice focuses on the litigation of real estate and business matters, transactional negotiations and drafting of agreements, alternative dispute resolution, mediation, and arbitration including service as mediator, arbitrator or referee, and land use planning, and development.

PRICE LAW FIRM is founded by Harry I. Price and Lori W. Price who have over 25 years of real estate law experience in Santa Clara Valley. Matthew E. Coleman, who has rejoined Mr. Price after previously practicing with him at their former law firm, has successfully prosecuted and defended numerous litigation and arbitration matters.  Lori W. Price now serves as "Of Counsel" to the firm. We are privileged to zealously represent clients, whether individuals who find themselves at an emotional crossroads, or business entities experiencing a defining moment. Our mission is to assist clients in plotting their course through the complex terrain of California real estate law to a successful resolution.
