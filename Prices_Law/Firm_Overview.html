Firm Overview

We continuously strive to assist our clients in securing an expeditious resolution to obstacles that arise in commercial and residential real estate transaction and litigation matters. Our primary focus is delivering quality legal advice and representation in the full spectrum of California real estate law, including the following:

Litigation of Real Estate and Business matters concerning Sales, Escrows, Title Insurance, Broker Liability and Fiduciary Duties, Construction, Development, Secured Real Estate Financing, Landlord-Tenant, and Boundary Disputes;

Mediation, Arbitration and Alternative Dispute Resolution, including service as Mediator, Arbitrator or Referee;

Transactional Negotiations and Drafting, including preparation of Agreements for Sale, Lease, Construction, Development or Ownership of Real Property, Secured Real Estate Loan Documentation, Entity Formation, and Title and Close of Escrow requirements; and

Land Use, Planning, Rezoning, Subdivision and Development compliance of commercial and residential property, including appearing before Municipal and County government agencies to secure necessary approvals.
