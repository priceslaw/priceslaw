Practice Areas

Our objective is to assist our clients in achieving a positive resolution. Clients seeking real estate solutions turn to PRICE LAW FIRM to provide transactional and litigation expertise in the areas of real estate and business law.

We are committed to providing our clients with superior legal services by developing long-term relationships with our clients, by advancing the interests of the legal profession and our judicial system and by proactively participating in the communities in which we live and work.

We are committed to developing the firm's relationships with its clients by:

  1. Planning: Carefully planning and managing a client's matter so that the requisite services are provided in the quality and manner required, at the appropriate time, and at a reasonable cost;

  2. Structuring: Helping the client recognize what the legal system can and cannot do, the benefits and risks of alternative courses of action and the likely cost; and

  3. Objectivity: Implementing the lawful objectives of the client without compromising the independence and objectivity that is essential to discharging our professional responsibilities.

Our practice areas are limited to the following:

  1. Litigation of Real Estate and Business matters;

  2. Mediation, Arbitration and Alternative Dispute Resolution, including service as Mediator, Arbitrator or Referee;

  3. Transactional Negotiations and Drafting; and

  4. Land Use, Planning and Development.
