We are located in a charming brick building on Main Street in Los Altos. As you are coming from San Antonio Road, we are the second building on the right.


















Directions from 280--coming from the south:

  1. Take 280 north to the El Monte exit. There are two ramps on this exit, take the first one (East).

  2. Proceed to Foothill Expressway, and turn left;

  3. Turn right at the first light, S. San Antonio Road;

  4. Proceed to the second light, and make a sharp left-hand turn onto Main St., the intersection leading into downtown Los Altos;

  5. We are the second building on the right.

Directions from 280--coming from the north:

  1. Take 280 south to El Monte exit; there are two off ramps on this exit, take the second one (East) towards Mountain View and Los Altos (not the first one that goes into the foothills toward the Junior College);

  2. Proceed to Foothill Expressway, and turn left;

  3. Turn right at the first light, S. San Antonio Road;

  4. Proceed to the second light, and make a sharp left-hand turn onto Main St., the intersection leading into downtown Los Altos;

  5. We are the second building on the right.

Directions from 101:

  2. go toward El Camino Real (away from the Bay);

  3. Proceed past El Camino Real to the third light, an intersection leading to downtown Los Altos;

  4. Make an angled right-hand turn onto Main St., the intersection leading into downtown Los Altos;

  5. We are the second building on the right.

Directions from 237--coming from the east:
